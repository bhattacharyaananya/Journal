--- layout: default title: The Security Sandbox --- # The Security Sandbox Welcome to **The Security Sandbox**, my technical cyber security journal. Here I document hands-on experiments, investigations, and research across blue teaming, offensive security, digital forensics, and applied cryptography. This site is aimed at practitioners who want **deep, reproducible, and tool-driven** write-ups rather than surface-level summaries. ## Domains I Write About ### Blue-Team & SOC Engineering I cover topics such as: - Designing and tuning **SIEM pipelines** (Wazuh, Splunk) for high-fidelity detection. - Building **detection engineering content** (rules, correlation searches, dashboards). - **Threat hunting** workflows, from hypothesis creation to validation. - Practical **incident response** case studies based on real-world-style lab setups. [Explore Blue-Team/SOC posts →](/blue-team-soc/) --- ### Offensive Security & VAPT Here I focus on the offensive side of the kill chain: - Full **VAPT write-ups** and methodology breakdowns. - Custom **exploit scripts** and PoCs (when safe to share). - **Active defense** techniques like honeypots and deception environments. - **Active Directory** attack paths, misconfigurations, and lateral movement scenarios. [Explore Offensive Security posts →](/offensive-security/) --- ### Digital Forensics & Incident Response This section documents forensic investigations end to end: - **Memory forensics** and disk forensics labs. - Analysis of **host artifacts** (registry, browser, event logs, etc.). - **Network forensics** from raw `.pcap` to timeline and attribution. - **Blockchain forensics** for transaction tracing and fraud analysis. [Explore Digital Forensics posts →](/digital-forensics/) --- ### Research, Cryptography & Emerging Security For more research-oriented work, I write about: - **Cryptographic flaws**, side-channel considerations, and implementation pitfalls. - **Reverse engineering** binaries, protocols, and obfuscated logic. - Emerging areas like **post-quantum cryptography** and **hardware security**. - Experimental ideas, from **ML-based detection** to **blockchain security**. [Explore Research & Crypto posts →](/research-crypto/) --- ## How to Use This Site - Each post is designed to be **reproducible**: I include tooling, lab setup, and steps. - All offensive techniques are presented from a **defensive and educational** standpoint. - Use these write-ups as a **reference**, a **lab guide**, or inspiration for your own experiments. If you’re interested in the underlying code and automation (CI/CD, static site config, scripts), check out the repository behind this site.
